# The 5-Step B2B Website Sales Framework

Use this framework when executing the "Pitch & Proposal Writer" workflow. It guides how to structure communication, emails, and proposals for B2B Website Sales.

## Step 1: Prospecting & Pre-Audit (Khám phá & Bắt mạch)
Before engaging, run a mental or tool-based audit.
- **Identify:** Is their site slow? Does it look bad on mobile? Are there clear CTAs? Is the SEO strategy non-existent or chasing vanity metrics?

## Step 2: The "Aha!" Moment (Chỉ ra Nỗi Đau)
This is the hook for your outreach.
- **Do NOT say:** "Your website is ugly, let us build a new one."
- **DO say:** "Your clinic is currently missing out on ~500 high-intent local searches every month for '[Specific Urgent Condition]'. Because your site lacks a dedicated booking page for this condition, those patients are going to [Competitor Name]."

## Step 3: Pitching the Solution (Trình bày Giải pháp)
Sell the "Lead Generation Machine", not the website.
- **Focus:** Highlight that the new website will be designed specifically around "Conversion-led SEO".
- **ROI Angle:** Frame the cost of the website against the cost of lost business or inefficient Ads spend. "A $5,000 website that generates $20,000 in new patient bookings monthly isn't an expense; it's an investment."

## Step 4: Objection Handling (Xử lý Từ chối)
Anticipate common objections in your proposals:
- **"We already have a website."** -> "Yes, but it's acting as a digital brochure, not a 24/7 sales rep. Let me show you the traffic leak."
- **"It's too expensive."** -> Show them the CPC data. "Your competitors are paying $2 per click for these keywords. If we rank you organically for the long-tail variations, the site pays for itself in 3 months."
- **"SEO takes too long."** -> "Traditional high-volume SEO takes long. We target high-urgency, low-difficulty local keywords, which show results much faster."

## Step 5: The Handoff (Bàn giao)
Ensure the proposal clearly outlines what the development team will deliver:
- Conversion-optimized UI/UX.
- Architecture ready for Local SEO.
- Fast loading speeds and E-E-A-T compliant structures.
