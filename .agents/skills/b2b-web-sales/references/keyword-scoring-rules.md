# Keyword Scoring & Strategy Rules (Conversion-led SEO)

This document contains the specific methodology for evaluating and selecting keywords to pitch to B2B Website clients, particularly in the YMYL/Medical niche. Use this when generating the Intent-Keyword Generator workflow.

## The Paradigm Shift
- **Old Way:** Chasing high Volume (Search Volume) and ignoring Keyword Difficulty (KD). Result: Ranking is impossible, zero conversions.
- **New Way (Conversion-led):** Targeting Low Difficulty, Specific Intent, and High Urgency keywords. Traffic may be lower, but conversion to paying customers is significantly higher.

## Scoring Factors

### 1. Keyword Difficulty (KD)
- Reverse the typical logic. Easy keywords (KD 0-10) get the highest priority (10/10 points). 
- **YMYL Note:** Even if a tool says KD is low, if the Top 10 SERP is dominated by massive hospitals/government sites, it is practically very difficult. Target the "narrow gaps" (Local search).

### 2. Search Volume
- Do not overvalue Volume. High volume = high traffic but often low intent.
- Keywords with Volume = 0 but high CPC or obvious transactional intent MUST be prioritized.
- Focus on "Local Patient Intent" (e.g., specific district or street).

### 3. Search Intent (Crucial)
Rank intent from highest to lowest conversion probability:
1. **Local/Brand (10 points):** "Phòng khám [bệnh] ở [địa phương]". Highest booking probability.
2. **Cost/Doctor (8.5 points):** "Giá phẫu thuật [bệnh]", "Bác sĩ [tên] giỏi". Strong consideration phase.
3. **Solution/Action (7 points):** "Cách chữa [bệnh]", "Thuốc trị [bệnh]".
4. **Symptom (5 points):** "[Triệu chứng] là bệnh gì". Focus on the patient's pain, not medical jargon.
5. **Informational (2.5 points):** "[Bệnh] là gì", "Nguyên nhân". Lowest priority for sales.

### 4. CPC (Cost Per Click)
- High CPC (e.g., >20,000 VND) means competitors are paying heavily for this keyword and making a profit. This is a massive signal for "Commercial/Transactional Intent".

### 5. Urgency (Tính cấp thiết)
- Evaluate how quickly the user needs a solution.
- **High Urgency (10 points):** "Bí tiểu cấp", "Gãy răng". Needs immediate booking. Content must push them to a Hotline immediately.
- **Low Urgency (3 points):** "Phì đại tuyến tiền liệt nhẹ". Chronic issues. Patients will take months to decide.

### 6. SERP Features (Local Pack)
- If searching the keyword triggers a Google Map (Local Pack), add bonus points. Local SEO is the best weapon for private clinics/businesses against massive competitors.

### 7. Keyword Length (Long-tail)
- Keywords with >= 4 words often show highly specific needs, are easier to rank for, and convert better. Use these to build "Topical Authority".

## The Ultimate Formula for Pitching
When presenting a strategy to a client, highlight keywords that fit this formula:
**Niche Long-tail Keyword + Local/Cost Intent + High Urgency + Low Volume = REAL PAYING CUSTOMERS.**
