# YMYL Keyword Scoring & Classification Framework

This framework is derived from high-converting SEO practices specifically tailored for medical, clinical, and YMYL (Your Money or Your Life) niches. It shifts focus from generic vanity traffic (high volume, low conversion) to **Conversion-led SEO** (low difficulty, high intent, high urgency).

---

## 📐 The YMYL Keyword Scoring Formula

Calculate the prioritization score for each keyword using this weighted formula:

$$\text{Total Score} = (\text{Volume Score} \times 10\%) + (\text{Intent Score} \times 50\%) + (\text{KD Score} \times 40\%)$$

*Note: You can adjust this to a 20% Volume / 40% Intent / 40% KD weight depending on the client's current Domain Authority (DA).*

---

## 🗳️ 1. Search Intent Scoring (50% Weight)
Classify and score keywords based on the patient's decision-making journey. Highest points go to terms closest to booking an appointment.

| Patient Intent Group | Score | Typical Modifiers / Examples | Conversion Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Local / Brand / Location** | **10** | "phòng khám", "ở đâu", "gần đây", "bệnh viện", "[Tên Thương Hiệu]" | **Immediate Booking** |
| **Consideration / Doctors / Cost** | **8** | "giá bao nhiêu", "bác sĩ giỏi", "review", "chi phí phẫu thuật" | **High Intent to Buy/Book** |
| **Solution / Treatment** | **6** | "uống thuốc gì", "cách chữa tại nhà", "phương pháp điều trị" | **Warm Prospect** |
| **Symptoms / Anxiety** | **4** | "tiểu buốt", "bí tiểu", "đau quặn bụng dưới", "són tiểu khi cười" | **Early Diagnostic Search** |
| **Information / Definition** | **2** | "là gì", "nguyên nhân", "phì đại tuyến tiền liệt là bệnh gì" | **Generic Traffic / Awareness** |

## ⚡ 2. Inverted Keyword Difficulty (KD) Scoring (40% Weight)
**Rule:** The easier the keyword is to rank, the higher the score it receives. Do not waste a client's budget fighting for KD 70+ terms at the start.

| KD Range (Ahrefs/Semrush) | Difficulty Level | Score | Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **0 - 10** | Very Easy | **10** | Prioritize immediately for quick traffic & fast conversion wins. |
| **11 - 30** | Easy | **8** | Include in primary SEO roadmap batch. |
| **31 - 50** | Medium | **6** | Build supporting cluster content first. |
| **51 - 70** | Hard | **4** | Address only after establishing Topical Authority. |
| **71 - 100** | Very Hard | **2** | Avoid in early stages unless critical for brand presence. |

---

## 📈 3. Search Volume Scoring (10% Weight)
Do not depend heavily on high search volume. High-volume terms often lead to low conversions. Low-volume or even zero-volume long-tail terms (e.g., "địa chỉ mổ phì đại tuyến tiền liệt uy tín tại Hà Nội") generate actual patients.

*   **Volume > 5000/mo:** 10 points
*   **Volume 2000 - 4999:** 8 points
*   **Volume 1000 - 1999:** 6 points
*   **Volume 300 - 999:** 4 points
*   **Volume < 300 (or "0" on tools but has high CPC):** 2 points (Add +3 bonus points if CPC is high, indicating commercial value).

---

## 🚀 4. Critical YMYL & Conversion Modifiers
Apply these extra rules to select "golden" keywords that win clients over:

1.  **Tính Cấp Thiết (Urgency Coefficient):**
    *   *High Urgency (10 points):* "Bí tiểu cấp tính", "són tiểu không tự chủ ra quần", "đau quặn thận". Bệnh nhân đang chịu đựng cơn đau dữ dội → Cần book lịch hoặc gọi Hotline lập tức. Giao diện web phải tối ưu số điện thoại nổi bật.
    *   *Chronic Urgency (3 points):* "Phì đại tuyến tiền liệt lành tính". Bệnh tiến triển chậm, bệnh nhân sẽ mất hàng tháng để nghiên cứu, đọc review trước khi quyết định khám.
2.  **Đặc Tính SERP (SERP Features - Local Maps):**
    *   If a keyword triggers a Google Local Map (Local Pack) on the search result page, prioritize it (+2 points bonus) and optimize Google Business Profile (Google Map) integration on the website.
3.  **Long-tail Preference (Word Count >= 4):**
    *   Long-tail keywords express precise needs, have lower competition, and lead to much higher conversion rates. Award higher priority to detailed phrases (e.g., "khám són tiểu sau sinh ở đâu tốt").
4.  **The YMYL & E-E-A-T Gold Rule:**
    *   Any page targeting these keywords **must** satisfy medical authority: reviewed/written by a real doctor with credentials, citing reputable medical sources, and clearly stating clinic licenses.
